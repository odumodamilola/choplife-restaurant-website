## 1. Brand Identity & Tone

**Brand Personality:** Vibrant. Warm. Bold. Cultured. Modern.
**The "Choplife" Vibe:** More than eating — a celebration of life through premium West African flavors.
**Tagline:** Bold West African flavors, beautifully served.

**Copywriting Voice:**
- **Voice:** Confident, warm, and clear.
- **Tone:** Conversational but high-end. We use "we" and "you" to build connection.
- **Language:** Avoid complex jargon. Focus on the *feeling* of the food. Simple, human, emotionally engaging.

## 2. SEO Content Hierarchy

### Page: Homepage (Primary Target: "Best West African restaurant in Osogbo")
- **Target Keywords:** Nigerian food Osogbo, fine dining Nigeria, Jollof rice restaurant, authentic African food
- **Meta Title:** Choplife Bistro — Bold West African Flavors, Beautifully Served in Osogbo
- **Meta Description:** Choplife Bistro brings the soul of West African cooking to a world-class dining experience. Explore our menu of 50+ dishes — from smoky Jollof to luxe Seafood Okra. Visit us in Osogbo.

### Page: About (Primary Target: "Choplife Bistro story")
- **Target Keywords:** West African food culture, traditional recipes modern twist, Nigerian restaurant brand
- **Meta Title:** Our Story — The Heart and Soul of Choplife Bistro

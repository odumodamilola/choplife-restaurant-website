# Choplife Bistro: Content & SEO Strategy

## 1. Brand Identity & Tone
**Brand Personality:** Vibrant, Modern, Welcoming, Cultured.
**The "Choplife" Vibe:** It's about more than eating; it's about the celebration of life through premium West African flavors.
**Copywriting Voice:** 
- **Voice:** Confident, warm, and clear.
- **Tone:** Conversational but high-end. We use "we" and "you" to build connection.
- **Language:** Avoid complex culinary jargon. Focus on the *feeling* of the food.

## 2. SEO Content Hierarchy

### Page: Homepage (Primary Target: "Modern West African Restaurant")
- **Target Keyword:** West African Culinary Experience, Fine Dining Osogbo, Authentic Nigerian Food.
- **Meta Title:** Choplife Bistro | The Ultimate West African Culinary Experience
- **Meta Description:** Experience West Africa on a plate. From smoky Jollof to luxe Seafood Okra, Choplife Bistro brings architectural precision to every meal. Book your table today.

### Page: About (Primary Target: "Choplife Brand Story")
- **Target Keyword:** West African Food Culture, Traditional Recipes Modern Twist.
- **Meta Title:** Our Story | The Heart and Soul of Choplife Bistro
- **Meta Description:** Born from fire and heritage. Learn how we merged traditional West African recipes with modern bistro elegance to create Choplife.

### Page: Services/Menu (Primary Target: "African Food Menu")
- **Target Keyword:** Nigerian Food Menu, Premium Party Platters, Best Egusi Soup.
- **Meta Title:** Curated Menu | Taste the Best of West African Cuisine
- **Meta Description:** Explore our architecturally crafted menu. Featuring traditional African dishes, luxury platters, and signature drinks.

## 3. Blog Content Strategy (SEO Articles)
1. **Title:** "The Secrets to the Perfect Smoky Jollof Rice"
   - **Angle:** Practical tips + highlighting our own recipe.
   - **Category:** Culinary Secrets.
2. **Title:** "Why Seafood Okra is the New King of Fine Dining"
   - **Angle:** Exploring the luxury side of traditional dishes.
   - **Category:** Food Culture.
3. **Title:** "A Guide to West African Spices You Need to Know"
   - **Angle:** Educational and sensory focus.
   - **Category:** Guides.
4. **Title:** "Choplife: How to Host the Perfect Dinner Party"
   - **Angle:** Lifestyle tips using our platters as a solution.
   - **Category:** Lifestyle.

## 4. Internal Linking Strategy
- **From Hero:** "Explore Menu" -> links to `#african-dishes`.
- **From About:** "Read Our Story" -> links to `#about`.
- **From Dish Cards:** "Book Table" -> links to `#location`.
- **Anchor Text Examples:** 
  - "Discover our [authentic recipes](#african-dishes)"
  - "Visit our [Osogbo flagship](#location)"
  - "Try our [luxe platters](#platters)"

## 5. Conversion-Focused CTAs
- **Primary:** "Book Your Table"
- **Secondary:** "Explore the Menu"
- **Low-Friction:** "Talk to Us" / "Call Priority Line"

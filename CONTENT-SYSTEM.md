## BRAND TONE & VOICE GUIDE

### Brand Personality

**Vibrant. Warm. Bold. Cultured. Modern.**

Choplife doesn't whisper. It welcomes you like an old friend, pulls out a chair, and sets something beautiful in front of you. The brand is confident but never arrogant. High-end but never cold. African at its core, global in its ambition.

### Voice Rules

| Do This | Not This |
|---|---|
| "We bring bold West African flavors to your table." | "We leverage culinary synergies to optimize the dining experience." |
| "Come hungry, leave happy." | "Our value proposition centers on customer satisfaction metrics." |
| "Every plate tells a story from our kitchen." | "Each menu item is strategically curated for maximum impact." |
| "You deserve food made with love." | "Our offerings are designed to exceed consumer expectations." |

### Tone in Practice

- **Homepage**: Confident, inviting, exciting. Like a friend saying "You have to try this place."
- **About Page**: Warm, human, storytelling. Like sitting with the founder over a meal.
- **Menu/Services Page**: Clear, appetizing, easy to scan. Like a waiter who knows every dish.
- **Blog**: Helpful, casual, knowledgeable. Like a food-loving friend sharing tips.
- **Contact Page**: Friendly, reassuring, simple. Like "We're right here, come say hi."

### Words We Use

- **Soulful, bold, vibrant, heritage, crafted, generations, community, home, warmth, celebration, flavors, roots, table, welcome, love, fire, tradition, modern**

### Words We Avoid

- **Synergy, leverage, optimize, utilize, scalable, deliverable, algorithm, funnel, ROI, stakeholder, bandwidth**

## CONVERSION FRAMEWORK

### CTA Hierarchy

| Level | CTA Text | Where Used | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary (High Intent)** | "Book Your Table" | Homepage hero, menu bottom, contact | Drive reservations |
| **Primary (High Intent)** | "Call Us Now" | Contact, location section | Immediate action |
| **Secondary (Medium Intent)** | "Explore the Menu" | Homepage, about page, blog | Drive menu engagement |
| **Tertiary (Low Friction)** | "Talk to Us" | Contact, footer | Low-pressure outreach |
| **Tertiary (Low Friction)** | "Read Our Story" | Homepage, footer | Build trust |
| **Micro CTA** | "View More" / "See Full Dish" | Dish cards | Drive deeper exploration |

### Emotional Triggers Used

| Trigger | How We Use It |
|---|---|
| **Trust** | "Quality Assured", "Established 2025", generations of taste, community-first language |
| **Belonging** | "There's a seat at our table for you", "Built for the culture", inclusive language |
| **Nostalgia** | "Takes you back to mama's kitchen", heritage references, cultural pride |
| **Curiosity** | "Taste what everyone's talking about", featured dish highlights, "you might also vibe with" |
| **Ease** | "Your table is one call away", simple CTAs, transparent pricing |
| **Celebration** | "More than a restaurant — a celebration", "we don't just serve food, we serve moments" |

### Conversion Path

```
Discovery (Google/Social) 
  -> Homepage Hero (clear value + CTA)
    -> Menu Exploration (appetite + interest)
      -> Dish Details (deep engagement + desire)
        -> Book Table / Call (action)
```

**Alternative path:**
```
Blog Article (SEO traffic)
  -> Internal link to Menu
    -> Featured Dish
      -> Book Table / Call
```
